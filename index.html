About us

Established with a passion for two-wheelers, Pannashree Automobiles has been serving bikers and enthusiasts for years, providing top-notch maintenance, repair services, and a wide range of genuine spare parts. We started our journey exclusively with Bajaj motorcycles, but over the years, we have expanded to cater to all major brands.

At Pannashree Automobiles, we understand the thrill of the ride and the importance of a well-maintained bike. Our team of skilled technicians are dedicated to keeping your motorcycle running smoothly, ensuring you can enjoy every journey with confidence. Whether it’s routine servicing, complex repairs, or sourcing hard-to-find parts, we’ve got you covered.
